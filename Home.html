Who am I?

I'm a 3rd year PhD Student in Applied Mathematics at INRIA and Université de Bordeaux. I am part of the MONC Team who develops mathematical solutions to oncology problems in collaboration with physicians. My thesis mainly focuses on medical data processing (segmentation, registration, etc).
